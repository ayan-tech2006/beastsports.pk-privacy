Delete Your Beast Sports Account

Last updated: July 5, 2026

If you would like to delete your Beast Sports account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to beastsports.pk@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request", including:

  • The email address or phone number registered with your Beast Sports account
  • Your full name (as registered on the app)
We will process your request and permanently delete your account within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon verified request, the following data associated with your account will be permanently deleted:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address
  • Account credentials and login information
  • Game activity, points, and referral history

Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be retained for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes even after account deletion, such as:

  • Order and transaction records required for tax and accounting compliance
  • Records necessary to comply with applicable law

Contact Us

For any questions regarding account or data deletion, contact us at:

Email: beastsports.pk@gmail.com
