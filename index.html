Privacy Policy for Beast Sports

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Beast Sports ("we", "our", "the app") is a sports e-commerce application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Beast Sports, we may collect the following information:

  • Name – to personalize your account and process orders
  • Email address – for account creation, login (OTP verification), and order updates
  • Phone number – for order confirmation and delivery communication
  • Shipping address – to deliver your purchased products
  • Order and payment information – including Cash on Delivery details or online payment transaction records (we do not store your full card/bank details; these are processed securely by our payment partners)
  • App usage data – such as game scores, points earned, and referral activity within our Games & Rewards system
  • Device information – basic device data used for app performance and advertising purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To send order confirmations, updates, and OTP verification codes
  • To operate our Games & Rewards system, including points, referrals, and prizes
  • To show relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To improve app performance and fix issues

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate Beast Sports:

  • Supabase – for authentication and database storage
  • Google AdMob – for displaying ads within the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy
  • Payment processors – for handling online payment transactions securely

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is shared only with the third-party services listed above, solely to provide app functionality (order processing, delivery, authentication, and advertising).

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your data is stored securely using Supabase's authentication and database infrastructure.

6. Your Choices

  • You can update or delete your account information by contacting us
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings

7. Children's Privacy

Beast Sports is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
